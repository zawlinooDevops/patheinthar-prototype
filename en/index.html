About Us

We are a business producing and distributing high-quality ready-to-eat seafood based on fresh local freshwater and marine fish and shrimp. We hold internationally recognized HACCP, GMP, and SOP Certificates, ensuring strict production standards for safe consumption.

To save consumers time and provide the best taste, we produce cleaned seafood like Squid Flower, Squid Head, and Baby Octopus, as well as ready-to-fry items like Breaded Shrimp, Fish Finger, and Shrimp Cutlets, alongside delicious Dimsum with high hygiene standards. Freshness, cleanliness, and safety are our primary values.
